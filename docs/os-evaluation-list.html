Method

Method

The SSF score uses a weighted 100-point model: Secure 40, Simple 30, Fast 30. Scores are interpreted with hard fail rules and declared scope.

Secure

App isolation, system integrity, network exposure, update model, privilege boundaries.

Simple

Coherent system model, low cognitive load, simple setup, permission clarity.

Fast

Low idle cost, bounded background services, responsiveness.

About

About this document

This evaluation extends the SSF concept into a stricter external benchmark. It is intentionally willing to say “not approved” even for strong systems when they fail the total doctrine.

Developed by: Obsidian

All systems were evaluated under the same SSF criteria, without adapting the framework to individual platform strengths.
